Magic Kingdom
is the first-built of the four theme parks at the Walt Disney World Resort.
It opened to the public on October 1, 1971.


Epcot
is the second of four theme parks built at the Walt Disney World Resort.
It opened to the public on October 1, 1982, and spans more than twice the size of Magic Kingdom.


Disney's Hollywood Studios
is the third of four theme parks built at the Walt Disney World Resort.
It opened to the public on May 1, 1989.


Disney's Animal Kingdom
is the fourth of four theme parks built at the Walt Disney World Resort.
It was dedicated and opened to the public during Earth Day on April 22, 1998.
